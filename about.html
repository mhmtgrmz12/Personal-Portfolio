About Me

My background, ambitions, and personal strengths as an Applied Computer Science student.

My Journey & Ambitions

I've been drawn to computers for as long as I can remember — what started as a curiosity about how networks and hardware work has grown into a broader passion for building things that actually solve problems. That's what led me to study Applied Computer Science at Thomas More, and why I'm specializing in Data and AI going forward.

Alongside my studies, I've been working professionally in tech for a while now. At Boticum, I develop automation systems and AI-powered chatbots for businesses — mostly around lead tracking across social platforms and personalizing client interactions. At FED Digital Agency, I work as a Technical Manager and Full Stack Developer, building websites and CRM systems for clients remotely from Belgium.

Long term, I want to build my own IT consultancy. I'm in no rush — I'd rather spend a few years working on hard problems and learning from good teams first.
