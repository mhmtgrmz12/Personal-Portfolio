My Journey & Ambitions

Ever since I was a child, I have been deeply fascinated by computers and the endless possibilities of technology. While my initial passion leaned heavily toward networking, I quickly realized that I thrive on understanding the broader technological landscape. I love exploring how different systems, hardware, and software connect to function as a whole. This drive for versatile, comprehensive knowledge is exactly why I chose to study Applied Computer Science. Moving forward in my academic journey, I have decided to specialize in Data and Artificial Intelligence, as I strongly believe this field will shape the future of our digital world.

As for my future, my primary ambition is to start my career working at a prestigious technology company. I want to tackle complex, real-world problems and gain invaluable industry experience. Ultimately, my dream is to leverage that solid foundation to establish my own IT consultancy firm, providing innovative and comprehensive technological solutions to businesses.
